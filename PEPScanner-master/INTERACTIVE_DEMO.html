üîÑ Screening Workflow

Customer Onboarding

Customer data is entered into the system either manually or through API integration. The system captures essential information including name, date of birth, nationality, and identification documents.

Automated Screening

AI algorithms perform comprehensive screening against multiple databases including PEP lists, sanctions databases, and adverse media sources using advanced fuzzy matching techniques.

Risk Assessment

Machine learning models analyze screening results and assign risk scores based on match quality, source reliability, and historical patterns.

Alert Generation

High-risk matches automatically generate alerts that are routed to appropriate compliance officers based on risk level and organizational hierarchy.

Review & Decision

Compliance officers review alerts, conduct enhanced due diligence if required, and make final decisions on customer acceptance or rejection.

Reporting & Documentation

All decisions are documented with audit trails. Suspicious activities automatically generate SAR/STR reports for regulatory submission.
